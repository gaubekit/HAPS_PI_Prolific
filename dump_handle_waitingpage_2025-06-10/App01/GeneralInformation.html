In this study, we will be investigating how people make decisions in different situations.
The study consists of three stages and will take about 45 minutes.

In this study, you will form a team with two other participants. Your team will remain the same throughout the study.
This means that your team will always consist of the same members.

In Stage 1, you will be asked to answer various questions. Some of your answers are already relevant for Stage 2 and Stage 3.

In Stage 2, you will join a team and get to know your team members. As part of this, you will participate in a 7-minute virtual team meeting. Furthermore, you will make a decision regarding your intended collaborate with this team.

In Stage 3, you will be asked to answer more questions. In the very end we inform you about your additional payout you earned in Stage 1 and Stage 2.
