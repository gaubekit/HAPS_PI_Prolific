On the next page, you will find six sliders (see example below), which ask you to indicate your preference about how to allocate bonus payoff between yourself and another anonymous person.

  • We refer to this person as the other.
  • All of your decisions are completely confidential.
  • There are no right or wrong answers, please simply decide according to your personal preferences.

Please consider the example below, and assume for demonstration reasons, that you decide to allocate 45 ECU to you and 50 ECU to the other:

In this example, the decisions you and the other made, would result in a bonus of 85 ECU for you and of 100 ECU for the others.

However, your bonus payoff for Stage 1 is the average of six such decisions.
