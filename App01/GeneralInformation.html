This study consist of three stages:
  • In stage 1, you answer questions and earn additional payout. You can proceed at your own pace.
  • In stage 2, you will join a team, get to know your team members for a joint 7-minutes virtual meeting and earn additional payout. You and your team members proceed in the same pace.
  • In stage 3, you will be asked to complete three questionnaires. You can proceed at your own pace.
Additional Payout:
Your additional payout is determined by your choices and the choices of your team members in stage 1 and stage 2. It will be explicitly state which answers to witch questions affect the additional payout.

In case there are any technical problems with the team(-forming) process in stage 2, you will be compensated for the additional time you spent in the study.

You can proceed in seconds.
