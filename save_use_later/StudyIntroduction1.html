Before you join a team with other people on prolific, we would like to introduce the Weakest Link Game.

Imagine that you have to work with the two other people over the next week on a joint project in virtual team , meaning that you will have regularly video meetings but also spend hours out of your 40-hour workweek to contribute to the project. You must choose how to allocate your time between the team projects, and other project.

Project A: This project is a team project. The success of this project depends on all members in your virtual team and is calculated by the contribution of the team member who contributes the least.

Project B: Think of this project as your own project. It has been assigned to you by the firm and you are solely responsible for the success.

We will ask you how many hours you allocate to Project A (denoted by hA). We also ask the others how many hours they allocate to Project A. Both, your answers and the answers of the others, will affect your payout.

The available choices are 0 hours, 10 hours, 20 hours, 30 hours, and 40 hours. Your remaining hours will be put towards Project B. For example, if you devote 30 hours to Project A, this means that 10 hours will be put towards Project B.
