This study consists of three stages:
  • In Stage 1, you will be asked to answer initial questionnaires individually, and earn some bonus payoff.

  • In Stage 2, you will join a team with two other participants, get to know your team members in a 7-minutes video meeting, perform some tasks, and earn some bonus payoff.

  • In Stage 3, you will be asked to complete questionnaires individually.
