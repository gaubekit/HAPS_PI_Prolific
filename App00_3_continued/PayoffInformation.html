Your payoff consists of a fixed compensation and a bonus payoff. The payoff scheme is as follows:

    Fixed payoff:
    • If you not complete the study or drop out at some point, you will receive a fixed compensation of £1.50.
    • If you complete the study, you will receive a fixed compensation of £4.

    Bonus payoff in Stages 1 & 2:
    • In Stage 1, you can earn a bonus up to £3,58, and in Stage 2, you can earn a bonus of up to £8.
    • We guarantee for a bonus of at least £2.
    • Your bonus in these Stages will depend on the decisions you and others made.
    • Detailed information about the payoff structure will be provided in subsequent pages.
    • These detailed information will use the Experimental Currency Unit (ECU).
    • For the payoff ECU will be converted into GBP (£): 10 ECU = £0.20.

    Compensation for waiting time:
    • After Stage 1, if we are not able to match you within 5 minutes with a team for Stage 2, you will have the opportunity to extend the waiting time by 5 additional minutes and be compensated with additional of £0.3 (max. 4 times = £1,2).
    • If we are not able to match you within 25 minutes, or you decided to not extend the waiting time, you will receive no bonus for Stage 2 and continue with Stage 3.

    Compensation for Team dropout:
    • If a team forms and somebody else drops out during Stage 2, we can not assign you a bonus for Stage 2.
    • In this case, you will receive instead a compensation of £3 and continue with Stage 3.

    Informing about payoff:
    • At the end of the study you receive detailed information about your payoff.
    • None of the participants will know how much another participant has been paid.

You can proceed in seconds.
