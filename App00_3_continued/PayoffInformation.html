Your payoff consists of a (A) fixed and a (B) bonus component. You will be (C) compensated for problems in the team-process.
Payoff = Fixed Payoff + Bonus Payoff + Compensation
The payoff scheme is as follows:

    (A) Fixed payoff:
    • If you do not complete the study, your fixed payoff will be £1.50.
    • If you complete the study, your fixed payoff will be £4.

    (B) Bonus payoff:
    • Your bonus depends on decisions you and others make in Stage 1 & 2.
    • Detailed explanations for the potential bonus is given on the respective pages.
    • For the bonus, we use the fictitious Experimental Currency Unit (ECU).
    • Conversion: 10 ECU = £0.2
    • You can earn in Stage 1 up to £3.58 (179 ECU) and in Stage 2 up to £8 (400 ECU).
    • We guarantee for a bonus of at least £2.10 (105 ECU).

    (C) Compensation:
    • We ask you in the team forming process (up to 4 times) whether you want to wait additional 5 minutes for Stage 2.
    • We compensate each 5 minutes additional wait time with £0.3 (max. £1.2).
    • If you decide to not wait any longer, or we are unable to match you after the 4th extension, you skip Stage 2 and proceed directly with Stage 3.
    • Dropouts during Stage 2, which are not caused by you, will be compensated with £3 (instead of the bonus for Stage 2).

We consistently update your payoff during the study. At the end, you receive detailed information about your final payoff. None of the participants will know how much another participant has been paid.

You can proceed in seconds.
